Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Picksy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Picksy does not collect, store, or process any personal data directly.

However, certain information may be provided voluntarily by you, including:

  • Feedback you submit through email
  • Optional contact information you choose to provide

3. Feedback and Third-Party Services

Picksy does not use any third-party services to collect or process user data.

If you choose to contact us via email, any information you provide will be used solely to respond to your inquiry.

4. How We Use Information

Any information you provide is used solely for:

  • Improving the app experience
  • Fixing bugs
  • Responding to user inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage

We do not store personal data on our own servers.

Any information you choose to send via email is not stored beyond what is necessary to respond to your request.

6. Your Rights

  • Choose not to provide any personal information
  • Contact us to request deletion of submitted information (if applicable)

7. Children’s Privacy

Picksy does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

picksy.feedback@gmail.com

10. Consent

隐私政策

更新日期：2026年4月

1. 引言

感谢你使用 Picksy。我们非常重视你的隐私。本隐私政策说明在你使用本应用时，我们如何处理相关信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

Picksy 不会主动收集、存储或处理任何个人数据。

但你可以自愿提供以下信息：

  • 通过电子邮件提交的反馈内容

Picksy 不使用任何第三方服务来收集或处理用户数据。

如果你通过电子邮件联系我们，你提供的信息仅用于回复你的问题。

你提供的任何信息仅用于：

我们不会在自己的服务器上存储任何个人数据。

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。所有更新将发布在本页面。
